Our Heritage

Where the
Atlantic
meets artistry

Nestled in the heart of Tórshavn — the world's smallest capital — Østrom is a sanctuary of Faroese artistic heritage. We curate and create handicrafts that carry the soul of these dramatic islands, from wind-swept cliffs to the deep northern sea.

Each piece in our collection is handcrafted with intention, celebrating traditions while embracing a contemporary sensibility that speaks to collectors and connoisseurs worldwide. When you hold a piece from Østrom, you hold the Faroe Islands themselves.
